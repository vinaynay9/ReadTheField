READ THE FIELD

Read the Field generates probabilistic player projections using advanced analytics and predictive modeling. Our system simulates thousands of potential outcomes to produce full probability distributions rather than single point predictions.

Each projection includes uncertainty quantification through percentile outcomes (10th, 50th, and 90th percentiles), allowing you to understand the range of possible results and make informed decisions under uncertainty.
